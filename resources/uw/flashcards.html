Flashcard Decks

Flashcards are the easiest way for me to retain information. At this point, I don't take notes for my classes anymore. It's all flashcards all the way, baby. I use the Anki software to create them, so the links below are to .apkg files. If you have Anki downloaded on your computer, double-clicking the downloaded file should open them up automatically; if not, right-click and select "open with".

I know a lot of people prefer using Quizlet, and yeah there's a way to import these decks into Quizlet, although idk about what that'll do to the cards with images or cloze deletions. But message me and I'll show you how to convert them!

  • AFM 102 (uWaterloo). See my review for the course here.
  • AP BIOLOGY (Advanced Placement). Actually this is a combined deck for both AP Bio and the Alberta Bio 20/30 curriculum. There's a lot of overlap between these two courses and I was taking them simultaneously, so I didn't bother separating the two at the time.
  • CHEM 120 (uWaterloo). For CHEM 120 only, not CHEM 120L!! (Although there is overlap between the finals, this deck does not provide comprehensive coverage of exam content for CHEM 123L.) See my review for the course here.

  • ECON 101 and 102 (uWaterloo). Including this deck is kind of questionable because... uh... it's pretty messy. It's a combined deck for Micro and Macro.
