# usage

## food

### spices

* ground black pepper: 8 months to use up a 150 g bag
* chili powder: 9 months to use up a 150 g bag
* ground paprika: 10 months to use up a 150 g bag

### staples

* all purpose flour: 6 months to use up a 10 kg bag
* butter or ghee: 5 months to use up a 1.6 kg jar

## toiletries

* hand soap: 1 year to use up a 250 mL bottle
* spf face lotion: 11 months to use up a 89 mL bottle
